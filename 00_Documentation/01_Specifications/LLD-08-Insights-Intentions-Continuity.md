## Privacy
Muhasaba and intentions are owner-only; admins cannot read them. Continuity board is opt-in and uses approved anonymous aliases. Raw repetition counts, email, real name, location and private text are never public.

## Weekly report
Aggregate completed core windows, continuity days, optional ward completion and neutral trends. No moral judgment or detailed private counts.

## Intention
One private active weekly intention per user/week, editable by owner, not public and not scored.

## Eligibility
Private completion is always preserved. Duration checks affect public eligibility only. Exact lower tolerance is a product-validation TBD; start wide and validate against anonymized distributions.

## Board projection
Expose only alias, high-level continuity score, eligible completion indicators and rank. Define deterministic ties.

## Gentle return
After interruption present the next meaningful action without catch-up pressure or permanent-loss framing.

## Tests
Opt-out removal; admin privacy denial; no raw counts in API; fast completion remains private completion; weekly/timezone boundary; deterministic ranking; neutral language review.
